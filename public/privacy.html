We use Google OAuth and email/password authentication provided by Supabase Auth. We do not store your Google password.

Payments

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain your login session. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies, but this may prevent you from using some features.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
