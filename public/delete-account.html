Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 2026

You may request deletion of your LandlordLedger account and associated data at any time. We process all deletion requests manually to ensure security and verify the request comes from the account owner.

How to Request Deletion

Email us from the address associated with your LandlordLedger account:

landlordledgersupport@gmail.com

Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address registered with your account in the body of the message.

What Gets Deleted

Within 30 days of receiving your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • Your business profile
  • All properties you have added
  • All transaction records (income and expenses)
  • All recurring transaction schedules
  • All notes (general and per-property)
  • Any active or past subscription records associated with your account

What May Be Retained

For legal, tax, and fraud-prevention reasons, we may retain the following for up to seven (7) years:

  • Payment and billing records required by tax law (handled by our payment processor, Stripe)
  • Anonymized usage logs that contain no personally identifiable information

Cancel Your Subscription First

If you have an active paid subscription, please cancel it before requesting account deletion. Cancellation can be done from the Billing tab inside the app. Deleting your account does not automatically refund any prepaid period.

Questions

If you have questions about the deletion process, reply to your deletion request email or contact landlordledgersupport@gmail.com.
